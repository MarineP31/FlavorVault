Delete Your Account

How to permanently delete your FlavorVault account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the FlavorVault app
  2. Go to the Profile tab (bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down to the Danger Zone section
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm the first prompt
  6. Confirm the final prompt by tapping "Delete Forever"

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently and immediately removed from our servers:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All your recipes
  • All recipe images
  • All meal plans
  • All shopping list items
  • All custom categories and tags

This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, there is no way to recover your data.
