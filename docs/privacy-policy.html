Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

FlavorVault ("we", "our", "the app") is a recipe management mobile application. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and an encrypted password. We do not collect your name, phone number, or any other personal information.

User-generated content: Recipes, meal plans, shopping lists, categories, and tags that you create within the app are stored in our database and associated with your account.

Recipe images: If you upload photos for your recipes, these images are stored in our cloud storage and associated with your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To authenticate your account and keep your data secure
  • To sync your recipes and data across your devices

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the app's core features.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure, which uses PostgreSQL databases with Row-Level Security to ensure that only you can access your data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only accessible to you through your authenticated account.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data (recipes, meal plans, shopping lists, categories, tags, and images) is permanently and immediately deleted from our systems.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through the app
  • Modify your data at any time through the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data from the Profile tab in the app. See our account deletion page for details.

7. Children's Privacy

FlavorVault is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
